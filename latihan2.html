Cat

Cats, also known as domestic cats or house cats (scientific name: Felis silvestris catus or Felis catus) are a type of carnivorous mammal of the Felidae family. The word "cat" usually refers to domesticated "cats", but can also refer to "big cats" such as lions and tigers, which are also a type of cat.

Today, cats are one of the most popular pets in the world. Cats whose bloodlines are officially recorded as pure breeds, such as persian, Siamese, manx and sphinx.
